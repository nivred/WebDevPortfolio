    Project Name:

    Corporate Creativity Coach

    Description:

    The Coach is a public speaker that uses a variety of methods to facilitate higher performance in team members within business and education. In an effort to better communicate to potential clients, the website needed to be brought up to speed with contemporary web practices. To preserve corporate appeal the redesign applied a very structured layout with neutral grays as a base. A small amount of color, graphics, and photos were added to make points of interest stand out in a way that displayed its own creativeness.

    Development and Design Tools:

    HTML > CSS > JavaScript > jQuery > Illustrator > Photoshop

    Project Name:

    AfriCaribe

    Description:

    AfriCaribe is a non-profit organization dedicated to education and awareness of Caribbean and African culture through the arts. Its website required a redesign that would not only update its look to modern standards but also reflect its spirit. The colors used within the layout were chosen to reflect the vibrancy of its performers and compliment traditional garb. Every effort was made to make the overall look welcoming and attention grabbing by implementing exciting images, a rotating gallery, videos and appealing graphics.

    Development and Design Tools:

    HTML > CSS > JavaScript > jQuery > Illustrator > Photoshop
